ABOUT ME

My name is Yngwie Johanne M. Punsalan. I'm a sprouting-newbie web developer whom has a keen interest in bringing digital ideas to life. I specialize in utilization of native tools, transforming prototype-based designs into captivating and engaging websites. My area of expertise at present lies in front-end development where I write clean code grounded with solid design principles I picked up from skilled front-end professionals during my past internship experience.

Although front-end is my current niche, I also have a solid foundation when it comes to backend development. When I first stepped and delved into web development, backend was my GOAT. Developers from my past internship experience were kind enough to help me understand how systems function behind the scenes. And so, I learned the few basic things: creation and utilization of databases, establishment and integration of the connection, and implementation of CRUD functionalities while also taking consideration of data integrity and security to avoid data breaches and unauthorized access.

Despite having an internship experience from a tech company, I'm still a college student currently taking up the Diploma in Information Technology program at Polytechnic University of the Philippines. With continuous learning and practice, I aspire to be a full-stack developer, turning digital concepts into practical solutions.
